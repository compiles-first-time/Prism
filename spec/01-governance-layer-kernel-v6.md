## Purpose

Expand the 20 kernel V6 governance gaps (GAP-102 through GAP-124, excluding GAP-108/GAP-114 which are primary in other specs) into implementation-ready SR rows. These SRs are ADDITIVE — they implement Track B (kernel) governance. All existing Track A SRs (SR_GOV_01 through SR_GOV_78) remain unchanged.
